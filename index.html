FAQ

FAQ

Q: What's the difference between Aloe Vera Water with Pulp and Aloe Vera Extract?
A: AloeVita Aloe Vera Water with Pulp is an everyday beverage that has 5% Aloe Vera content by volume. The Water with Aloe Pulp is a better-for-you functional beverage compared to other popular carbonated, bottled juices, and soft drinks. This tasty beverage is meant to be enjoyed everyday because it tastes good and makes you feel good. AloeVita Aloe Vera Extract is 99% Pure Aloe Vera and is a dietary supplement for health and well-being. The extract is commonly used to aid in digestive relief.

Q: Where is the Aloe Vera from?
A: AloeVita owns and operates all of the Aloe Vera farms used to make AloeVita drink products. All of the Aloe Vera is certified organic by ECOCERT®. Our Aloe Vera farms are located in the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and we process the leaves within 24 hours after harvesting to assure the highest concentration of Aloe Vera vitamins, minerals, and amino acids go into every bottle.

Q: Who makes AloeVita?
A: AloeVita owns and operates all of the bottling facilities that are used to make AloeVita. The facilities are located within close driving proximity from our Aloe Vera farms assure that we process the Aloe Vera immediately after harvest. The facilities are certified Organic by ECOCERT®.

Q: Do you pasteurize this product?
A: Yes. Our machinery uses a patented method to extract the Aloe Vera then filter and flash pasteurize the water at 120 degrees for up to five seconds.

Q: How long after I open AloeVita does it stay fresh?
A: We suggest that if you open AloeVita and do not finish drinking it, that you place it in a refrigerator and drink it within 24-48 hours.

Q: How much AloeVita should I drink?
A: We know customers enjoy drinking AloeVita because it makes them feel good. Aloe Vera is packed with vitamins and nutrients so we recommend you drink as much as you would like.

Q: Is AloeVita gluten free and vegan-approved?
A: Yes to both: our AloeVita is gluten free and vegan-approved, as AloeVita does not contain any animal-derived or dairy products in its beverages. it is also low sodium and fat free.
